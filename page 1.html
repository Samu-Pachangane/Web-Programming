Traditional Art and Craft: A Window to the Past

-by Sameeksha Pachangane

Welcome to my blog on traditional art and craft, where I explore the rich cultural heritage and techniques that have been passed down through generations. From intricate wood carvings to delicate pottery, traditional art and craft are a testament to human creativity and skill.

In this blog, I will delve into the world of traditional art and craft, highlighting the different techniques, materials, and artists that have contributed to this rich cultural landscape. Whether you are an art enthusiast, a craft hobbyist, or simply someone who appreciates the beauty of traditional craft,
I hope you will join us on this journey of discovery.




The crafts of India are diverse and depict rich history, culture, religion, and civilization. The craft of the different states of India reflect the influence of different cultural patterns and hold a strong story behind its origin. For over decades, crafts have been rooted as a symbol of culture and tradition within different communities of India.
